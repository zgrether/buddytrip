# Privacy Policy

**Effective date: 07/14/2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how BuddyTrip ("BuddyTrip," "we," "us," or "our")
collects, uses, and shares information when you use the BuddyTrip application
and website at bbmi.app (the "Service").

We built BuddyTrip to help groups plan trips and run friendly competitions. We
collect only what we need to make that work, we don't sell your data, and we
don't serve ads.

> **Note:** This is a plain-language policy for a small group-trip application.
> If you have questions, contact us at zgrether@gmail.com.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information you provide

- **Account information.** When you create an account, we collect your name and
  email address. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email
  address, and profile picture from Google (via your Google account's basic
  profile — "email," "profile," and "openid").
- **Trip and planning information.** Content you create or contribute in the
  app, including trips, crew/roster details, destination ideas and votes, date
  poll responses, schedule and lodging details, quick-info notes, and expense
  entries and splits.
- **Competition and scoring information.** Team assignments, game and match
  setup, and scores you enter (golf scores, match/hole outcomes, and related
  results).
- **Messages.** Trip and team chat messages you send within the app.

### Information about people you invite

When you invite someone to a trip, we create a placeholder ("guest") record
using the name and/or email you provide, so they can be added to the trip
before they create their own account. If that person later signs up with the
matching email, their guest record is merged into their new account.

### Information collected automatically

- **Basic technical information** needed to operate the Service, such as
  authentication tokens and session information.
- We use standard infrastructure logging from our hosting and database
  providers (see "Service Providers" below). We do not use advertising trackers.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and operate the Service (create accounts, manage trips, run
  competitions, deliver chat and notifications);
- Authenticate you and keep your account secure;
- Send you Service-related email, such as trip invitations;
- Maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the Service;
- Respond to your requests and provide support.

We do **not** sell your personal information, and we do **not** use it for
third-party advertising.

---

## 3. How We Share Information

### With other users of your trip

BuddyTrip is a group application. Information you contribute to a trip — your
name, RSVP and voting responses, scores, expenses, and trip-channel chat
messages — is visible to other members of that trip. Team-channel chat messages
are visible only to members of your team.

### With service providers (sub-processors)

We rely on a small number of third-party services to run BuddyTrip. These
providers process data on our behalf:

- **Supabase** — database, authentication, and file/data storage (hosts your
  account and trip data).
- **Google** — sign-in, if you choose to sign in with Google (Google provides
  us your basic profile information; your use of Google sign-in is also subject
  to Google's own privacy policy).
- **Resend** — sending transactional email such as invitations.
- **Vercel** — application hosting and delivery.

We also retrieve golf course information (such as hole pars and yardages) from a
third-party course-data provider ([golfapi.io]) to populate scorecards. We
request course data from this provider; we do not send your personal
information to it.

### For legal reasons

We may disclose information if required to do so by law, or if we believe in
good faith that disclosure is necessary to comply with a legal obligation,
protect our rights or the safety of others, or investigate potential violations
of our terms.

### If BuddyTrip changes hands

If BuddyTrip is involved in a merger, acquisition, or transfer of assets, your
information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will note any
such change here.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored with our database and hosting providers (primarily
Supabase). We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including
access controls and row-level security policies that restrict data access to
the appropriate trip members. No method of transmission or storage is completely
secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to
provide the Service. When you or a trip owner deletes content (such as a trip,
expense, message, or competition), that content is removed from our active
database. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal
information by contacting us at zgrether@gmail.com.

Some information may remain in routine backups for a limited period, and we may
retain information where necessary to comply with legal obligations or resolve
disputes.

---

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

- **Access** the personal information we hold about you;
- **Correct** inaccurate information (you can edit much of your profile and trip
  content directly in the app);
- **Delete** your personal information;
- **Object to or restrict** certain processing.

To exercise any of these, contact us at zgrether@gmail.com. You may also manage or
revoke Google sign-in access from your Google account settings.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

BuddyTrip is not directed to children under 13, and we do not
knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has
provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

---

## 8. International Users

BuddyTrip is operated from the United States. If you
access the Service from elsewhere, your information may be transferred to,
stored, and processed in a country with different data-protection laws than
your own.
